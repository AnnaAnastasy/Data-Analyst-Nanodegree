Act Report¶

Act Report communicates the insights and displays the visualizations produced from wrangled data while doing the project (saved as 'wrangle_act.ipynb').

Final dataset after wrangiling used for analysis and visualization contains 1450 rows and 13 columns. The most important columns for analysis are: retweet_count, favorite_count, rating, breed_prediction and name.

Twitter users' favorite and the most retweeted dog breeds¶

As we can see, there is a strong positive correlation between favorites and retweets by users. It's not suprising as users often retweet and like the same photos.

Top 3 favorite dogs are:¶
  • Saluki
  • French Bulldog
  • Afghan Hound
Top 3 retweeted dogs are:¶
  • Standard Poodle
  • English Springer
  • Afghan Hound

WeRateDog's favorite breeds¶

Here we also can see positive correlation between favorites by users and by WeRateDogs but it's not as strong as the one between favorites and retwets by users.

According to WeRateDogs, the most adorable and highly rated dog breed are:¶
  • Saluki
  • Briard
  • Tibetian Mastiff
  • Border Terrier
  • Silky Terrier

An interesting fact is that the most favorite dog is the same for both users and WeRateDogs. It's Saluki.
